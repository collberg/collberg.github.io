Consulting and Training

I am available for consulting and training in all areas of software protection and reverse engineering.

ISSISP. For the last 10 years, together with my colleagues Bjorn De Sutter (Gent), Jack Davidson (Virginia), Roberto Giacobazzi (Verona), and Gu Yuan (IRDETO), I have been organizing the International Summer School on Information Security and Protection: Beijing (2010), Gent (2011), Tucson (2012), Xi'an (2013), Verona (2014), Rio de Janeiro (2015), Amrita (2016), Paris (2017), Canberra (2018), and Qingdao (2019)..

LigerLabs is a set of lectures I am making available to the public at https://ligerlabs.org.
